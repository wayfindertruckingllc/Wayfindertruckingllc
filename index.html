About Wayfinder Trucking

Wayfinder Trucking LLC is a Florida-based trucking and logistics company built on discipline, second chances, and a vision for long-term growth in freight transportation.

Our Services

We plan to provide box truck freight, dry van trucking, dispatch support, carrier setup assistance, and logistics solutions for businesses needing dependable transportation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a reliable trucking company while creating lawful employment opportunities for people rebuilding their lives after incarceration.

Growth Vision

Wayfinder Trucking is starting with a lean operation and building toward a fleet of 10–15 trucks, dispatch services, carrier partnerships, and a strong Florida freight presence.
